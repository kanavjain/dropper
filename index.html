Stim Webtoys Library

Discover a variety of interactive visual experiences that are meant to engage your senses and curiosity. Explore each webtoy to see what resonates with you.

3D Toy

Explore a surreal 3D tunnel with a twisting torus knot, dynamic lighting, and vibrant particles, all in sync with sound.

Star Guitar Visualizer

Take a visual journey through procedurally generated scenery, inspired by an iconic music video, synced to your music.

Defrag Visualizer

A retro-style, sound-reactive visualizer that recalls the look and feel of old-school defragmentation processes.

Evolutionary Weirdcore

Watch as a weirdcore-inspired landscape evolves with fractals, glitches, and surreal visuals that react to music input.

Multi-Capability Visualizer

A versatile visualizer that responds to both audio and device orientation, creating dynamic shapes and effects in real time.

Trippy Synesthetic Visualizer

A kaleidoscopic world of patterns that respond to both audio and microphone input, offering a rich synesthetic experience.

Pattern Recognition Visualizer

A visualizer that uses pattern recognition to generate objects that move dynamically with sound.

SVG + Three.js Visualizer

A hybrid visualizer combining SVG effects and Three.js for a unique experience that reacts to audio in real time.

Dreamy Spectrograph

A music visualizer blending WebGL with 2D visuals to create a dreamy spectrograph that moves with your audio.

Interactive Word Cloud

Generate a word cloud that interacts with your voice input, making words float and shift based on the sounds around you.
